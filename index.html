What happens when we show up

There are 3 Tornado T-3000 tables for play. They're currently $1 loonie to play a game. There's a $5 entry fee that goes towards the pot that gets paid out at the end of the night.

We do 2 hours of qualifying, playing as many rounds as we can, and then go into a single-elimination bracket with the players that did have the best results of the night. Usually will be top 8 but if we get over 20 players, we will do top 16.

We alternate between Monster DYP and Swiss DYP. If you don't know what these are, come on out a bit earlier on one of the days we meet and we'll go over them with you in a bit more detail.

Monster DYP - Draw Your Partner - Every round during the qualifying rounds, you'll get a new partner from whomever else is playing that night. You'll generally get to play with and against all the players that are there for the night.

Swiss DYP - Draw Your Partner - At the start of the event, you will be partnered up with a random player who shows up for the event and you will be playing with that person for the entire night.
